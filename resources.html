How to talk to your children about the Pandemic

If you are overwhelmed and your child sees you as very anxious, perhaps intolerant, and angry, talk to him/her when they are calmer. Admit that you were angry, scared, or upset, but that you are better and that in the end everything will be fine.

Remember

Children will react to new situations according to the reaction of the parents; so, they will react to the pandemic according to the emotional reaction of the parents. They are carefully observing the emotional signs of the parents to evaluate the seriousness of what happens.
